GoHodl.it

Important

  • Make sure the retrieval date is correct. Once you send your tokens, there's no way to retrieve them until after that date.
  • Only ERC20 tokens are supported. ETH is not supported.
  • Only browsers and plugins are supported, for example: Metamask, Brave. No private keys. Ledger support very soon!
  • Zero fees. You only have to pay the GAS cost for sending/recieving your tokens. This is just a fun project.
  • When you send tokens to hodl, it will create two transactions. One to approve the contract to transfer the tokens, and the other to send them to the contract.
  • Most importantly, happy hodling!
